Checklist for AWS Course: Cloud Practitioner (Foundational)

April 1, 2025

I’ve recently started studying to get a certificate in AWS. The certificate that I’m working towards is AWS Cloud Practitioner (Foundational), but there are a lot of different certifications within the umbrella of AWS that may suit your needs better. This probably won’t be the last certificate I work towards within the scope of AWS anyhow; I'm always trying to expand my knowledge!

For this course, there’s a lot of free material, but I feel like it’s organized pretty poorly. However, I’m the type of person who prefers clear goals in a visual or written format. To make this easier for myself, I decided to go ahead and create a checklist of sorts in order to better organize the material that I’m trying to learn. Note that all of the lessons under step two with the exception of the SimuLearn and all of the lessons under step three can be found under the title “Standard Exam Prep Plan: AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C02)” at https://explore.skillbuilder.aws/learn/learning-plans/2170/standard-exam-prep-plan-aws-certified-cloud-practitioner-clf-c02.

While this checklist may not suit your needs and doesn’t currently include paid material, I thought that I would share it in case someone else found it helpful.
